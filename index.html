Key Features & Structure

Powered by Geyser-Spigot, our server supports both Java and Bedrock players simultaneously. Join from any device and play with your friends.

Explore our world in real-time with BlueMap, a high-performance 3D web map. Find bases, plan projects, and see the community's creations.

Official Server Rules

Welcome to DamMinecraft! Our goal is to create a fun, fair, and long-lasting community.
Please read the rules carefully before joining.

Section 1: Community Respect & Chat Rules

This is the most important section. We are a community first.

  • Be Respectful: Absolutely no harassment, bullying, racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, or any other form of bigotry or hate speech. This is a zero-tolerance policy.
  • No Spamming: Do not spam the chat with repeated messages, excessive capital letters, or strings of random characters.
  • Keep it PG-13: Avoid excessive swearing. Do not engage in overly sexual or graphically violent discussions.
  • No Sensitive Topics: To avoid conflict, do not discuss sensitive real-world topics like politics or religion.
  • Respect Staff: Listen to and respect the decisions of Admins and Mods. Arguing with staff after a final decision has been made is not productive and may result in a mute.
  • No Advertising: Do not advertise other Minecraft servers, Discord servers, or personal social media without permission.
Section 2: Griefing, Stealing & Pranks

Protecting your builds and items is our top priority.

  • No Griefing: Do not destroy, deface, or otherwise damage another player's build. This includes breaking blocks, using lava/water/TNT, or damaging the landscape around a base.
  • No Stealing: Do not take items from another player's chests, item frames, armor stands, or shulker boxes. If it's not yours, don't touch it.
  • Farms: Do not use another player's farm without their permission. If you are given permission, you must replant any crops you harvest.
  • Pranks: Pranks are allowed only if they are 100% non-destructive. A "prank" that involves TNT, lava, fire, or breaking any blocks is considered griefing. You must clean up your own prank within 48 hours.
Section 3: Hacking, Cheats & Exploits

We are a "vanilla-ish" server. Fair play is mandatory.

  • No Hacked Clients: Any client that gives you an unfair advantage is forbidden (X-Ray, Kill-Aura, Flight, etc.).
  • No X-Ray: Using X-Ray texture packs or mods is considered cheating.
  • No Exploits: Do not abuse any game or server bugs (duping, seed cracking, etc.). If you find a bug, report it to staff immediately.
  • Allowed Mods: Client-side performance mods (Optifine, Sodium), Litematica (schematic-only), and Mini-maps (no player/cave display) are generally allowed. Ask staff if unsure.
Section 4: Building & Environment

Be a good neighbor and keep the world clean.

  • Respect Base Boundaries: Do not build your base within 200 blocks of another player's claimed base without their explicit permission.
  • Spawn: Do not build private bases or large farms within 500 blocks of Spawn (0,0). This area is for community builds.
  • Clean Up Your Mess: Do not leave floating treetops, 1x1 "nerd poles," or random patches of destruction.
  • Claiming: Clearly mark your base and property lines with signs or fences.
Section 5: Server Performance & Lag

Help us keep the server running smoothly for everyone.

  • Redstone: All complex redstone contraptions must have an on/off switch and be turned off when you are not using them.
  • Entity Limits: Do not create massive, overcrowded animal farms (e.g., no more than 50 of one animal type per chunk).
  • Mob Farms: Large-scale mob farms must be approved by staff before construction.
  • No Lag Machines: Intentionally building devices to lag or crash the server will result in a permanent ban.
Section 6: PvP (Player vs. Player)
  • Consensual PvP Only: PvP is disabled by default. You may only engage in combat if all players involved clearly agree to it in chat.
  • No "Trolling": Do not repeatedly kill a player or trap them in a death loop. All items must be returned after a consensual fight unless agreed upon beforehand.
Section 7: Staff & Rule Enforcement

How to get help and what happens if you break a rule.

  • How to Report: If you have been griefed or stolen from, do not retaliate. Take screenshots, note the coordinates, and report it to a staff member privately.
  • Enforcement: Breaking rules will result in a warning, kick, temp-ban, or permanent ban, depending on the severity.
  • Staff Will NOT: Spawn items, teleport you (unless stuck), or give you ranks.
